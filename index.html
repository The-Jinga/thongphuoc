About us

THONG PHUOC PRODUCTION TRADING COMPANY LIMITED is a leading manufacturer and exporter of high-quality cashew nuts in Vietnam. With years of experience in the cashew industry, we are committed to delivering premium products, competitive prices, and excellent customer service to our global partners.

Our production facility is equipped with modern technology and strict quality control processes to ensure that our cashew nuts meet international standards such as ISO, HACCP. We specialize in processing and supplying various cashew nut grades, including WW320, WW240, WW450, LP, SP, and more.

At Thong Phuoc, we prioritize sustainability, ethical sourcing, and customer satisfaction. Our mission is to bring the finest Vietnamese cashews to the world while maintaining long-term partnerships with our valued clients.

We welcome importers, distributors, and business partners worldwide to collaborate with us for mutual growth and success.

Company History

Founded in 2018, THONG PHUOC PRODUCTION TRADING COMPANY LIMITED started with a vision to become a trusted supplier of premium Vietnamese cashew nuts to the global market. With a strong commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, we have steadily grown into a reputable manufacturer and exporter in the cashew industry.

From humble beginnings, we invested in modern production facilities, advanced processing technologies, and strict quality control systems to ensure that every product meets international standards such as ISO, HACCP. Over the years, we have built long-term partnerships with importers, distributors, and wholesalers worldwide, earning their trust through consistent quality, competitive pricing, and professional service.

Today, Thong Phuoc continues to expand its market reach, delivering the finest Vietnamese cashew nuts while maintaining our core values of integrity, sustainability, and excellence. We look forward to further growth and collaboration with partners worldwide.

Our Brand

HAGI is a Vietnamese brand specializing in premium roasted and seasoned cashew nuts. Sourced from the finest cashew-growing regions of Vietnam, our products are crafted using a blend of traditional roasting techniques and modern processing technology, delivering naturally flavorful, crunchy, and rich-tasting cashews.

HAGI offers a wide variety of products, from classic salted roasted cashews to bold flavored ones such as garlic chili, wasabi, cheese, and cacao – meeting the diverse preferences of consumers worldwide.

Guided by the motto "Quality is our foundation – Flavor is our identity," HAGI is committed to innovation and excellence in bringing Vietnamese cashews to the global market.
